Welcome to Bright Insight - Support Network!

 

Founded in 2018 by therapist Patty Williams and public-school teacher Tori Jones, Bright Insight - Support Network takes a whole-person approach to educating about, advocating for, and supporting the gifted, twice-exceptional, and neurodivergent communities. As a grassroots organization, Bright Insight seeks to create a network of individuals, communities, and resources to collectively support exceptional individuals in all environments.

Our Mission

Bright Insight - Support Network’s mission is to educate, advocate for, and encourage gifted, twice-exceptional, and neurodivergent persons, while creating a network of support through which the needs of this unique population can be identified and met.

Our Vision

Bright Insight - Support Network’s vision is that through insight and empowerment, exceptional individuals and underserved populations will receive the support necessary to thrive.

Our Values

Bright Insight - Support Network is a neurodiversity affirmative organization that values Compassion, Congruence, and Creativity along with Unconditional Positive Regard for all people from all cultures and walks of life. Bright Insight - Support Network also seeks to maintain a growth mindset while focusing on individual and community strengths.
