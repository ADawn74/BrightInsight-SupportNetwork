Resources

 

 

Twice Exceptional/ 2E Network International
   "An international group of parents and professionals who are working to increase the recognition and support for [twice exceptional]individuals who are bright and also have challenges that affect their ability to learn, socialize, and/or be successful in life"

 

Hoagies' Gifted Education Page
   "The all-things-gifted site, full of resources, articles, books and links to help and support parents, teachers, and gifted children alike"

 

Dabrowski Center
   "A non-profit organization dedicated to making the world a better place by removing the stigma of mental illness and promoting an alternative approach to personality development"

 

Your Rainforest Mind: Support for the Excessively Curious, Creative, Smart, & Sensitive
   Books, musings, consultation and more from Paula Prober

 

National Association for Gifted Children
   "The nation's leading organization focused on the needs of gifted and talented children"

 

SENG- Supporting Emotional Needs of the Gifted
   "A nonprofit network of people who guide gifted, talented, and twice-exceptional individuals to reach their goals intellectually, physically, emotionally, socially, and spiritually"

 

Positive Disintegration Podcast
   "An exploration of positive disintegration through conversation and shared stories. It’s a framework for understanding a variety of intense experiences that often look and feel like mental illness, but which can also be viewed as building blocks for creating an authentic personality."

 

Davidson Institute
   "A robust organization that provides a community, opportunities, and resources for profoundly gifted young people and their families"

 

Gifted Homeschooler Forum (GHF)
   "Empowering gifted families to make strategic, proactive, and intentional educational choices"

 

With Understanding Comes Calm (2e Support)
   "Bringing out the best in 2e kids & adults"

 

William & Mary Center for Gifted Education
   "A research and development center providing services to educators, policy makers, graduate students, researchers, and parents in support of the needs of gifted and talented individuals"

 

SENIA – Special Education Network and Inclusion Association
   Advocacy and recources for indivudals with disabilities

 

Renzulli Learning - Total Talent Development
   "An interactive online system that provides students with a personalized learning environment, allowing teachers to easily differentiate instruction to increase engagement and achieve higher academic performance"

 

Gifted Unlimited, LLC
   "A publisher of quality, research-based information pertinent to the gifted population and to those who support them"

 

The G Word on Netflix
   "Love it or hate it, the government plays a huge role in our lives. Adam Conover explores its triumphs, failures and how we might be able to change it."

 

Gifted Development Center
   "A global leader in the assessment and development of gifted individuals of all ages"

 

Riley Morgan, AMFT
   Teletherapy to residents of California specializing in "providing neurodiversity affirming therapy for autistic adults and teens"

 

Gifted Support Network
   "[Providing] information, connection, and enrichment to families and educators of gifted, high-ability, and twice-exceptional children"

 

Bloomers: Gifted and 2e Adults Facebook Group
   "A social support group linked to the Bright Insight community page, where gifted community members can talk openly about giftedness and twice-exceptionality as adults and interested stakeholders."
